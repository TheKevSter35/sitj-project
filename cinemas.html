Cinemas

You can have a good time at some of Amsterdams great cinemas. Form the big cinemas like the Pathé Tunschinski to the small cinemas like The Movies the oldest cinema of Amsterdam.


Discounts

CJP Pass - if you are aged 29 or under then you may want to concider buying a CJP (Cultureel Jongeren Paspoort) pass, which gives you discount for Movies, Plays, Museuams. a CJP pass is yours for only €17.50. You can buy one ate the Amsterdam Uitburo, Tourist Informaite Offices and on the CJP Website. More Info about CJP check there website for available discounts: www.cjp.nl .

Pathé Tuschinski

Pathé Tuschinski, originally Theater Tuschinski, is a movie theater in the Netherlands in Amsterdam. It is considered to be one of the most beautiful cinemas in the world.
