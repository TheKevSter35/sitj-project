Cafes

Amsterdam's many eateries combine the atmosphere of a pub with the good food of a restaurant, making them good places to have a reasonably priced meal and meet people at the same time. Most of them serve daily or weekly specials, bargain diches on addition to the regular menu.

Fest

Cafe Fest is behind HvA Wibauthuis. They have coffe and tostis (grilled ham and cheese sandwiches) for you while you work on your laptop. Their dinner menu include salads and burgers and all this fot a student-friendly prices.
