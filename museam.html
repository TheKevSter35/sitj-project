Museums

Amsterdam has many great museums. The jewel in the crown is the rijksmuseam but there are many more that are worth a visit. Also, every year on the first Saterday in November, Amsterdam opens its museums during museums Night (Museamnacht). This one night event is very special and includes all kinds of activities in several participating museums.
